# Walkthrough - HumatiQ Use Case Diagram

I have designed a comprehensive and modular Use Case Diagram for the HumatiQ platform based on the provided backlog.

## Accomplishments

### 1. Actor Definition
Identified 3 primary actors (Candidate, Recruiter, Admin) and 3 secondary/system actors (AI Engine, LinkedIn API, MongoDB).

### 2. Modular Structure
Organized the system into 10 logical modules to ensure clarity and scalability:
- **Authentication & Identity**
- **Security & Access Control**
- **Recruitment Management (RECRUIT)**
- **Profile & Assessment (PROFILE)**
- **Analytics & Insights (INSIGHT)**
- **Psychological Synergy (PSYNARIO)**
- **Career Growth (GROW)**
- **Candidate Portal**
- **Compliance & Privacy (RGPD)**
- **Automated Notifications**

### 3. Modular PlantUML Split
Instead of a single cluttered diagram, I have generated **10 separate PlantUML blocks**, one for each module:
- **Authentication & Identity**: Login flows and registration.
- **Security & Access**: RBAC, MFA, and logs.
- **Recruitment (RECRUIT)**: Parsing, scoring, and job management.
- **Profile (PROFILE)**: 360 views and personality assessments.
- **Analytics (INSIGHT)**: KPIs and AI Chatbot.
- **Psychology (PSYNARIO)**: Team synergies and conflicts.
- **Growth (GROW)**: Training and mobility.
- **Portal**: Candidate dashboard and job tracking.
- **Compliance (RGPD)**: Data rights and consent.
- **Notifications**: Alerts and reporting.
